Implement the **FREE K-Shaman Reading result generation system** for the InnerMeter platform.

IMPORTANT:
Do NOT use external AI APIs.
All fortune results must be generated using deterministic rule-based logic based on the user's input.

Inputs available:

* birth date
* gender
* selected persona (애기보살 / 선녀보살 / 천신도령 / 무속인 / 법사)

The same input must always generate the same result.

---

## STEP 1 — CALCULATE CORE DESTINY VALUES

From the birth date, calculate the following values.

1. Destiny Turning Age
   Choose one from this list based on birth date hash:

28, 31, 33, 36, 39, 41

2. Success Age
   Turning age + random offset from:

+2, +3, +4

3. Strong Love Ages
   Generate 3 ages between 24 and 42.

Example:
26, 31, 38

4. Wealth Flow Period
   Generate a 5~7 year period after success age.

Example:
35 ~ 41

5. Warning Ages
   Generate 2 caution ages.

Example:
29, 34

초반 고생형

## STEP 4 — APPLY PERSONA SPEECH STYLE

The same fortune data must be expressed differently depending on the persona.

애기보살 style:
soft, intuitive tone
example phrases:
“음… 흐름을 보니”
“가만히 보니 이런 기운이 있네요”

천신도령 style:
direct and confident tone
example phrases:
“이 시기가 전환점입니다”
“여기서 길이 갈립니다”

선녀보살 style:
mystical and gentle tone
example phrases:
“운의 흐름이 부드럽게 움직입니다”
“좋은 기운이 이어집니다”

무속인 style:
traditional and serious tone
example phrases:
“사주 흐름을 보면”
“이 시기 이후로 운이 살아납니다”

법사 style:
warning-oriented and realistic tone
example phrases:
“이 시기에는 조심해야 합니다”
“판단을 서두르면 안 됩니다”
